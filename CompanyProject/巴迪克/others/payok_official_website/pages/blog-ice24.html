PAYOK Shines at ICE London 2024: Powering iGaming with Localized Payment Excellence

Feb 19, 2024

In February 2024, PAYOK made a resounding debut at ICE London, the world’s premier iGaming industry event, attracting over 40,000 professionals from 150+ countries. Held at ExCeL London, the expo provided a global stage for PAYOK to showcase its cutting-edge payment solutions tailored for the iGaming sector.

As a licensed MSB Canada (FINTRAC) provider, PAYOK emphasized its fraud prevention tools and instant settlement capabilities, critical for high-volume iGaming transactions.

PAYOK’s focus on localized payment service drew significant attention from operators seeking to streamline pay-ins and payouts in key markets like Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Europe. Demonstrations of region-specific solutions—including bank transfers in the most of Asia counties, PIX in Brazil, and Trustly integrations in all channels—highlighted PAYOK’s ability to bridge regulatory and operational gaps for iGaming platforms.

The event solidified partnerships with 50+ operators and reinforced PAYOK’s reputation as a trusted payment partner. “ICE London confirmed the demand for agile, compliant payment solutions in iGaming,” said Shan Mohammed, Head of Sales. “Our mission is to turn payment complexity into growth opportunities.”

With plans to expand into Africa and Europe, PAYOK continues to empower iGaming businesses to scale globally—one secure transaction at a time.
