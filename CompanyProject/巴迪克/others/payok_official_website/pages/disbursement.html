Key Features

Real-time Transfer

PAYOK is directly connected with licensed banks to ensure every disbursement is received in real-time without delay

Easy to Integrate API

The disbursement service is quickly integrated through API's to ensure speed and efficiency whatever the purpose

Convenient Dashboard

The PAYOK dashboard allows our partners to review transactions in detail. Merchants can also use this dashboard to execute manual disbursements, including batch disbursement

Reduce manual processes and errors

Say goodbye to manual money transfers and enjoy the convenience of disbursing up to 10,000 payments by simply uploading one Excel file

Easy Tracking

Enjoy the convenience of being able to track transactions in real-time, with reconciliation reports available for download from our dashboard

Reliability

Our customers enjoy a 99% success rate and the highest speed of transfer. Our intelligent system dynamically directs every transfer to the optimal bank in our network. If a transfer were to fail, our system would automatically redirect the transfer via the best alternative channel available
