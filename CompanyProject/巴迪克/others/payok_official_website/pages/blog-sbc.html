PAYOK Elevates Cross-Border Payments at SBC Summit Lisbon 2024

Sep 15, 2024

PAYOK took center stage at SBC Summit Lisbon in September 2024, Europe's premier iGaming and sports betting conference, held at the Feira Internacional de Lisboa. The event drew 12,000+ industry leaders, underscoring Lisbon's role as a bridge between European and Latin American markets.

PAYOK unveiled payment innovations, addressing the needs of operators targeting Southeast Asia and Latin American audiences. Live demos highlighted PAYOK's multi-currency wallets and real-time fraud detection tools, critical for high-risk iGaming transactions.

Our impactful participation at SBC Summit Lisbon 2024 not only promoted PAYOK's payment solution in iGaming sector but also forged strategic alliances with forward-thinking operators and regulators.

By showcasing how PAYOK turns regulatory complexity into competitive advantages, we reinforced our commitment to empowering operators with agile, secure, and localized payment infrastructure. The event's success reaffirms our vision: to be the catalyst for growth in markets where speed, compliance, and innovation are non-negotiable.
