PAYOK Redefines Gaming Payments at ICE Barcelona 2025

Feb 15, 2025

PAYOK made waves at ICE Barcelona in January 2025, the global gaming industry's flagship event, held at Fira de Barcelona. Drawing 35,000+ professionals, the expo spotlighted PAYOK's role in transforming payment ecosystems for casinos, sportsbooks, and iGaming platforms.

PAYOK's iGaming payment solutions are meticulously crafted to empower operators in high-potential markets like Southeast Asia and Latin America. By prioritizing reliability, cost-efficiency, and frictionless transactions, we ensure operators can seamlessly integrate trusted, region-specific payment channels that resonate with local audiences.

As demand surges for hyper-localized infrastructure in these regions, PAYOK's agile solutions are uniquely positioned to empower platforms scaling into these high-growth markets. Our expertise in compliance, fraud prevention, and real-time settlements enables iGaming businesses to focus on expansion, not operational hurdles.

The summit catalyzed 50+ partnerships, PAYOK continues to empower operators to scale confidently in regulated markets. ICE Barcelona confirmed that agility and security are non-negotiable in gaming. Our mission is to turn payment bottlenecks into growth accelerators PAYOK is poised to redefine how the gaming industry transacts—securely, swiftly, and without borders.
